# eduweb

eduweb is a "school of new technologies" focused on practical digital skills. It bridges the gap between theory and market needs, teaching both current industry standards and the emerging tech of tomorrow. The learning experience combines video courses, live meetings, and the active community.

Note: eduweb operates in Polish.

![](https://cloud.overment.com/eduweb-1764865683.jpeg)

## Content & Structure

The library hosts over 1000 hours of video material organized into **Learning Paths** and **Specializations**:
- **Programming:** Full-stack development, coding standards.
- **Design:** UI/UX, Web Design, No-code (Webflow, Framer).
- **Creative:** 3D Modeling, Motion Graphics, Audio/Video production.
- **Business:** Digital marketing, automation, and scaling.

**Key Features:**
- **AI Integration:** An intelligent assistant for summaries and code support.
- **Certification:** Skills validation upon completion.
- **Community:** Ahoy! provides networking and mentorship.
