## What it is

Suite of 10+ creator tools (one subscription) built on Stripe. Helps creators and businesses sell digital products, courses, subscriptions, tickets, and SaaS with high-converting checkouts and automated workflows. Go from idea to selling in 5 minutes.

## Core tools

- **Easycart:** High-converting checkout with 1-click payments, upsells, cross-sells, and recovery
- **Easylove:** Collect and display testimonials (text/video) with wall of love
- **Easyplayer:** Host and deliver courses and gated content
- **Easycookie:** GDPR-compliant cookie consent management
- **Easylegal:** Auto-updating legal documents (privacy policy, terms, refund policy, NDA)
- **Easyfaq:** Embeddable FAQ sections to reduce support load
- **Easyticker:** Live sales notifications to boost social proof
- **Easyoffer:** Product storefronts and pricing pages
- **Easytimer:** Countdown timers for promotions and launches
- **Easycoffee:** "Buy me a coffee" donation widget
- **Easypage:** Landing page builder (coming soon)

## Key features

- **1-click checkout:** Payment details saved across all Easytools merchants (network effect via Stripe)
- **Checkout recovery:** Automated abandoned cart recovery
- **Customer portal:** Self-service for subscriptions and purchases
- **Promotions & discounts:** Time-limited offers with countdown timers
- **Digital downloads:** Secure delivery of files and course content
- **Branding:** Full customization to match your brand
- **Integrations:** Discord, WordPress, Airtable, Pipedrive, HubSpot, Mailchimp, Make, Zapier, and more

## Why it works

- **70% lower fees** compared to Gumroad, Paddle, LemonSqueezy
- **47-65% checkout conversion rates** (testimonials from users)

- **No-code setup:** Launch in 5 minutes
